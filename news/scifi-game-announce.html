Exciting New Game Announcement, NR:IC!

Published on July 8, 2024 | By IamAppley

Nexo Games Studios is so excited to announce our original story adventure game, NR:IC, No Return: Invasion Chronicles.

Featuring a voxelated sci-fi city, set in the year 4000, an alien race known as the Solori, led by the unforgiving Shadowblight and his Synod of Truth, has taken over our fair home, Earth. Under their command, Earth may fall, or, with your help, may rise greater than it ever has before!

In this mystery sci-fi combat adventure game, players will get to experience the feel of your original favorite top down RPGs, in an all 3D voxelated style. While following along with an incredible story filled to the brim with combat, mysteries, breakthroughs, and more.

Key features of NR:IC include:

  • A Giant Voxelated Map With Missions All Around
  • Deep Character Development in an Incredible Story
  • Exciting Scenes and Lore
  • Puzzles, Dialogue, and Mini-Missions

We can't wait to share more details about No Return: Invasion Chronicles in the coming weeks. Stay tuned for development info, gameplay previews, and more exciting announcements as we approach the release date.
