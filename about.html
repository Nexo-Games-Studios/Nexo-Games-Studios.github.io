About Nexo Games Studios

Nexo Games Studios is an indie game development studio dedicated to building the future of interactive entertainment through developing the very best games and telling the greatest stories for everyone to enjoy everywhere.

Inspired by studios like Telltale Games, Bungie and Mojang Studios, the idea for our studio was conceived in 2019, and unofficially from then to 2024, where we finalised our idea of No Return: Invasion Chronicles, our mainline adventure RPG, we have planned our expanse into the future to bring about the best gaming experiences imaginable.

At Nexo Games Studios, we leverage state-of-the-art technology, such as AI, and a custom game engine, to create unforgettable experiences.
